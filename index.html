Building My Raspberry Pi Air Quality Monitor

Building My Raspberry Pi Air Quality Monitor

This project was one of the most challenging yet rewarding experiences I’ve had. From picking the right sensors to learning how to read their data, every step pushed me to understand electronics and Python better. I faced lots of trial and error—wiring mistakes, calibration issues, and making the readings meaningful—but seeing it finally work was incredibly satisfying. The monitor can now measure air quality and categorize it as good, moderate, or bad, which taught me how coding and hardware can come together to solve real-world problems. This project didn’t just test my technical skills, it also tested my patience and problem-solving, and it’s something I’m proud to show in my portfolio.
